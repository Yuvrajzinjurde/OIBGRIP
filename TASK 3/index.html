The Celsius and Fahrenheit scales are both used to measure temperature, but they have different zero points and intervals between degrees.In most countries, the Celsius scale is used, while the Fahrenheit scale is used mainly in the United States.Knowing how to convert between Celsius and Fahrenheit is important in many fields, including science, medicine, and cooking.There are many online tools and apps available to make the conversion process easier.It's important to be familiar with both scales and know how to convert between them, especially when traveling or working with people from different countries.
